Welcome to Goderich Chess Club

Goderich Chess Club welcomes all chess enthusiasts to join us every Tuesday, 6-8 PM, at Goderich Library (52 Montreal St), and every Friday, 6-9 PM, at Goderich Legion. Admission / membership is free.

Whether you are a seasoned player looking to sharpen your skills or a newcomer eager to learn the game, this is the perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in the fascinating world of chess. Engage in fun and challenging matches while making new friends along the way.
