About Our Club

We are a volunteer-run, weekly community chess club meeting at the Goderich Branch of the Huron County Library (52 Montreal St) every Tuesday from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Open to all skill levels for free.

What We Offer

  • Casual games
  • CFC/FIDE rated matches
  • Tactics & Puzzles
